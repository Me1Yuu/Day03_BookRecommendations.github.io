Introduction

All hope seems lost. But Kelsier refuses to accept that. The young Skaa is one of the legendary "Mistborn" and possesses the magical power of Allomancy.

IKIGAI

Author: Francesc Miralles and Hector Garcia

Genre: Non-Fiction

Why I love it: This book combines philosophy and practical advice on how to find purpose and happiness in life. The concept of Ikigai is beautifully explained.

Favorite Quote: "The secret to a long life is to find your Ikigai." - Francesc Miralles

Three reasons why this book is awesome:

  • Practical Wisdom: The book provides actionable steps to discover your own Ikigai, making it relatable and applicable.
  • Beautifully Illustrated: The visuals enhance the reading experience and help convey the concepts effectively.
  • Inspiring Stories: The authors share real-life examples of people who have found their Ikigai, making it motivational.

Introduction

Ikigai is a Japanese concept that means "a reason for being." It is the intersection of what you love, what you are good at, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for.
This book explores the idea of Ikigai and how it can lead to a fulfilling and happy life.
The authors share stories from the people of Okinawa, known for their longevity, and how they embody the principles of Ikigai in their daily lives.

Big Panda and Tiny Dragon

Author: James Norbury

Genre: Illustrated Fiction

Why I love it: This book is a heartwarming tale about friendship and the journey of self-discovery. The illustrations are charming and complement the story beautifully.

Favorite Quote: "Sometimes, the smallest things take up the most room in your heart." - James Norbury

Three reasons why this book is awesome:

  • Beautiful Illustrations: The artwork is whimsical and adds depth to the story, making it visually appealing.
  • Heartfelt Messages: The themes of friendship, kindness, and self-acceptance resonate deeply.
  • Short and Sweet: The book is a quick read but leaves a lasting impact, perfect for all ages.

Introduction

Big Panda and Tiny Dragon is a story about two unlikely friends who embark on a journey of self-discovery.
They explore the world around them, learning valuable lessons about life, love, and friendship.
The book is filled with beautiful illustrations that bring the characters and their adventures to life.
